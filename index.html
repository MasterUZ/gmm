STAY HEALTHYFitness Support

Beyond weight loss support and convenient healthy meal options, GMM provides guidance on improving overall wellness. Sign up for free, and commit to a more active lifestyle with the help of GMM fitness experts and fitness club & personal trainers partners.

According to the Center of Disease Control (CDC) aerobic physical activity at a minimum of 2.5 hours per week coupled with 2 or more times a week of muscle-strengthening activities will yield health benefits. For even more results, double time to 5 hours per week of moderate intensity. People who commit to active lifestyles generally live longer, have a lower risk for heart disease, stroke, type 2 diabetes, depression, and some cancers.
