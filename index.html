EAT WELLMeal Plans

Yes, yummy can be healthy! Actually there is a science to it and our dietitian chef and executive chef painstakingly measure and balance our fresh, locally prepared meals so that your diet is always in line the recommendations from the Academy of Nutrition & Dietetics. In addition to saving you time in the kitchen, you will easily achieve a healthy amount of :

  • Health promoting Carbohydrates to support sustained release of energy throughout the day
  • Omega 3s for heart health
  • Protein
  • Fiber

We don’t believe in deprivation, so we support goals through a program that is as easy to maintain as an ongoing lifestyle choice. You will eat what you love AND achieve your health goals at the same time!

Running from carbs keeps you running on empty; running on empty can slow metabolism! According to the Center for Disease Control & Prevention (CDC), at least of 2.5 hours of aerobic activity per week, coupled with strength training activities, will yield health benefits. DO GOODCommunity Impact

Our nutrition coaches are all smiles because they are the vital link between Good Measure Meals™ & Open Hand Atlanta. As a Good Measure Meals™ customer, your dollars help bridge the gap between the socio-economic disparity that off-sets equal access to healthy food options.

Our dietitians are strengthening our community by implementing nutrition education programs and providing specialized consults for over 3,000 health-vulnerable individuals. With your help, Open Hand Atlanta delivers nearly 2 million medically-appropriate meals annually to our underserved and nutritionally at-risk neighbors.

We’re advancing towards a healthier community together, and your personal journey with Good Measure Meals™ provides the means for us to reach back and grab those in need to come along with us. Now that’s a reason to smile!
